About Košice Guide

Curated by people who actually live here — not generated by an algorithm.

Our Story

Košice Guide started the way most good things do: with frustration. We kept meeting travellers who'd been to Prague, Budapest, Krakow — and somehow Košice had passed them by entirely. Or worse, they'd rushed through it in a day, not knowing what they were missing.

We are a small team of Košice locals — a historian, a chef, a photographer, and a former city tourism officer. We've each spent years exploring every corner of this city, building relationships with the people who make it special, and figuring out what it actually means to experience Košice properly.

Košice Guide is the result. Every recommendation here is something we've personally tested, every place we've eaten at, slept at, or spent an afternoon in. No commissions, no algorithmic rankings, no " sponsored content." Just honest editorial judgment from people who care about this city.

How We Choose

Authentic, Not Touristy

We avoid the places that exist primarily to separate tourists from their money. We choose the places that locals actually go, and that reward curiosity.

Tested by Us

Every restaurant, hotel, bar, and experience in this guide has been personally experienced by a member of our team. We don't write about places we haven't been.

Editorial Independence

We accept no payment for inclusion. Our recommendations are based purely on quality and experience. If a place stops being good, it comes off the list.

Real Košice Identity

We celebrate what makes Košice distinctly Košice — its Slovak and Hungarian heritage, its Habsburg history, its position at the crossroads of Central Europe.
