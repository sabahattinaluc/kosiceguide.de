Things to Do in Košice

Beyond the sights — experiences that reveal the city's character.

Tours & Experiences

Tour Editor's Pick

Košice Walking Tour

A two-hour guided walk through 750 years of history with a local historian. Covers the cathedral, medieval walls, the plague column, and stories you won't find anywhere else.

Tour • Daily • 2 hours
Urban

Industrial Heritage Trail

A self-guided walk through Košice's 19th-century industrial past. The Iron Works, the Tatra paper factory, and the rail yards — the city was once the industrial engine of Eastern Slovakia.

Self-guided • Half Day • 4 km
Food

Food Market — Stará prístav

Košice's weekly food market at the Old Port. Local producers, street food, and a genuine sense of community. Saturdays 8am–2pm only.

Market • Saturdays • Stará prístav
Wellness

Aquacity Poprad Day Trip

A 45-minute drive from Košice to Slovakia's largest thermal spa resort. Year-round tropical pools, water slides, and a spa village. A perfect day trip.

Day Trip • 45 min • Poprad
Music

Košice Music Nights (Október)

A ten-day autumn music festival across twelve venues. Classical, jazz, electronic, and folk — Košice at its most alive. Book accommodation early.

Festival • October • Various venues
Nature

Tatra Mountains Day Hike

Košice is the gateway to the Low Tatras. A full-day hike to Chopok or Krížala offers some of the most accessible and spectacular alpine scenery in Central Europe.

Hiking • Full Day • 1.5 hrs drive

With Children

Family

City Park Children's Railway

A narrow-gauge railway run by children for children — a charming Košice institution. Adults are allowed if accompanied by a small person.

Family • City Park
Zoo

Košice Zoo

A well-run zoo with a particular strength in Central Asian species. The polar bear exhibit is unexpectedly impressive. Full day with the adjacent wildlife park.

Family • Full Day
