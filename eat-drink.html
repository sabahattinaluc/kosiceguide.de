Eat & Drink Košice

Traditional Slovak comfort food, international kitchens, and a craft beer scene that punches far above its weight.

Restaurants

Slovak Trending

Hostinec Šupica

Traditional Slovak food in a candlelit medieval cellar. Bryndzové halušky here is a revelation. Book at least three days ahead.

$$ • Slovak • Old Town
Contemporary

Východná

Refined Eastern European cuisine with a modern approach. Seasonal tasting menu and an exceptional natural wine list. A labour of love from a young Košice couple.

$$$ • Fine Dining • Old Town
Café

Kafe Káli

The best coffee in Košice, without question. Small-batch roaster with a genuine passion for provenance. Also does outstanding breakfast and all-day eggs.

$ • Café • Old Town
Slovak

Republika

An elegant all-day restaurant inside the Hotel Bankov. Slovak classics done with precision and beautiful local ingredients. The wild boar pappardelle is unforgettable.

$$$ • Slovak • Outskirts
International

Abácia

Mediterranean-inspired cuisine in a restored abbey. The garden terrace in summer is one of the most pleasant dining spaces in the city. Good cocktails too.

$$ • Mediterranean • Old Town
Italian

Pizzeria Da Vinci

Wood-fired Neapolitan-style pizza made with Slovak toppings where possible. Fast, casual, and consistently excellent. The truffle mortadella is a standout.

$ • Pizza • Old Town

Bars & Nightlife

Brewery

Schreier Brewery

Košice's oldest craft brewery, operating since 1627. Tours available, but the real treat is drinking fresh unfiltered lager in the courtyard on a warm evening.

$$ • Brewery • Old Town
Cocktail

Pivný Bar na streche

A rooftop bar above the Old Town with views of the cathedral. Excellent cocktails, decent wine list, and the kind of relaxed vibe that makes you want to stay for one more.

$$ • Cocktail • Old Town
Speakeasy

Miesto

Hidden behind an unmarked door in a courtyard off Hlavná. No sign, no menu on the wall — just exceptional cocktails and genuine atmosphere. Ask a local how to find it.

$$$ • Cocktail • Old Town
