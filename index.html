Why Košice?

A City with Soul

Košice is Slovakia's second-largest city and its cultural heartbeat. With a beautifully preserved medieval old town, a thriving arts scene, and some of the best food in Central Europe, it rewards those who look beyond the obvious destinations.

We've walked every street, eaten at every restaurant we recommend, and spoken with locals who share their city with generosity and pride. This guide is the result — curated, not algorithmic.
