Where to Stay

Properties we've personally vetted — no algorithmic recommendations, no generic chains.

Boutique & Unique

Historic Editor's Pick

Hotel Bankov

A 16th-century mining manor on a forested hill above the city. Twenty rooms, a Michelin-referenced restaurant, and a silence that makes it impossible to leave. Book at least a week ahead.

$$$ • Historic • Outskirts
Design

Hotel Yasmin

Košice's most contemporary hotel — clean design, excellent service, and a location that's equidistant to the old town and the business district. Consistently rated among the best in Slovakia.

$$$ • Design • Central
Apartment

Apartment Mlynská

A restored 1920s apartment in a heritage building directly on the main pedestrian street. Full kitchen, washing machine, and the kind of character that no hotel can replicate. Minimum three nights.

$$ • Self-catering • Old Town
Grand

Hotel Grand

The grandest hotel in Košice, a short walk from the cathedral. Recently renovated to a high standard while preserving its interwar elegance. The breakfast is exceptional.

$$$ • Grand • Old Town
Guesthouse

Penzión Slovakia

A family-run guesthouse in a quiet residential street ten minutes from the centre. Simple, clean rooms and a breakfast that locals still come to eat at even when they're not staying.

$ • Guesthouse • Residential
Business

DoubleTree by Hilton

Košice's international business hotel near the convention centre. Reliable, comfortable, and practical if you're in the city for work. The executive lounge is worth the upgrade.

$$$ • Business • Business District

Budget Friendly

Hostel

Hostel Alvater

A thoughtfully designed hostel in the Jewish Quarter. Excellent common areas, a proper kitchen, and staff who genuinely care about the city. The best social hostel in Košice.

$ • Hostel • Jewish Quarter
Budget

HotelOlympic

A solid mid-range option near the train station — practical if you're arriving late or departing early. Rooms are clean, the breakfast is generous, and the price is honest.
